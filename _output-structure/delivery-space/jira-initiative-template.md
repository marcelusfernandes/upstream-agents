## Best Practices

### Writing Effective Initiatives

1. **Be Strategic:** Connect to business objectives and user needs
2. **Be Specific:** Clear success metrics and validation criteria
3. **Be Realistic:** Honest about risks and dependencies
4. **Be Complete:** All information stakeholders need to understand and support
5. **Be Actionable:** Clear phases, epics, and next steps

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ Vague problem statements ("improve experience")
❌ Missing success metrics or unrealistic targets
❌ No risk identification or mitigation
❌ Unclear phases or timeline
❌ Not linking to supporting documentation

### Initiative vs Epic vs Story

- **Initiative:** Strategic goal, multiple epics, cross-team, months
- **Epic:** Feature set, multiple stories, single team, weeks
- **Story:** Single feature, single developer, days
